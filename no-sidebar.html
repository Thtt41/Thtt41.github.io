Our School

Nguyen Hue High School / THPT Nguyễn Huệ

Nguyen Hue High School - District 9, Ho Chi Minh City was established in 1997. Compared to other schools in the city, it can be said that this is an old school. With more than 20 years of construction and development, the school has made changes in teaching methods and facilities to ensure effective teaching and learning. The name of the school was taken from the name of a national hero with respect. All staff, teachers and students always keep in mind that they will strive to be worthy of the respected hero. The school's name has been kept the same since its establishment until today.

• Green area & architecture: With a spacious area, green - clean - beautiful design, helping children study in an airy environment close to nature.
• Classroom system & functions: The school has about 32 standard classrooms, along with 3 laboratories, audio-visual rooms, 4 computer rooms, a library, STEM – MOS room, multi-purpose rooms, multi-purpose house...
• Playground - sports: Full facilities such as: multi-purpose hall, 4 badminton courts, 2 volleyball courts, basketball court and swimming pool to help students have a place to exercise after school.
• Educational technology: The entire school is equipped with projectors and computers, integrating bilingual programs, STEM, MOS, etc., building a model of "intuitive and effective learning hours".

Nguyen Hue High School is an “educational highlight” in Thu Duc City with comprehensive training quality, a modern environment, a dedicated teaching staff and outstanding student achievements. Although it is a public model, the school still creates a balance between academics, talents and life skills—deserving of being the top choice for parents and students in the Eastern area of ​​Ho Chi Minh City.

Teaching staff - highly qualified and dedicated

The school has more than 131 staff members, including 1 doctor and 36 masters, most of whom have high professional qualifications and graduated from prestigious pedagogical schools. Teachers regularly innovate teaching methods through demonstration activities, topics, STEM and MOS projects, and at the same time guide students to compete for excellent students and develop soft skills.
Following the motto “Learning to be a good person – Learning to integrate”, the school focuses on training students in ethics and social responsibility before teaching knowledge.

Curriculum and extracurricular activities

Diverse training: Providing regular, integrated, specialized, sports talent systems with bilingual models (English, Japanese, French, German, Korean, Chinese).
STEM & MOS program: Integrating technology - digital skills into the curriculum, helping students access international standards such as MOS and creative thinking through STEM.
Abundant clubs & extracurricular activities: More than 23 clubs including Math, Physics, Chemistry - Biology, STEM, Information Technology, English, Basketball, Badminton, Modern Dance, Debate... creating a dynamic and creative environment.

Outstanding achievements & quality assurance

High school graduation rate has been 100% for many years. 2023-2024 school year: 100% good conduct, 99% good - excellent academic performance; won 29 city-level excellent student awards, 11 casio awards; Gold Medal for male/female volleyball at Thu Duc city level. The school achieved the title of "Excellent Labor Collective", achieved excellent student achievements and citywide sports movement.
